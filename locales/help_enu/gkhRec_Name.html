Personal names

The names are intended for storing in personal records all personal names, with varying completeness of indication of parts, in different languages, of different types.

Names are added on the tab of the same name in the personal record editing dialog.

Pressing the keys [ Ctrl + Down ] in the fields for entering the last name, married last name, first name and patronymic - leads to "unification" of the corresponding line, the first letters of the words become capital, the rest - lowercase.

Attention: using the "/" symbol in the name fields (last name, first name, patronymic) is prohibited, as this may lead to errors in the database.
